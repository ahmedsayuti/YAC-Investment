Our Products

We supply a wide range of petroleum and energy products to meet your business needs.

Gasoline (PMS)

Premium motor spirit for automotive engines

Our high-quality gasoline (PMS) is refined to meet international standards, providing optimal performance for all types of gasoline engines. We supply both regular and premium grades to meet your specific requirements.

Gas oil (AGO)

Automotive gas oil for diesel engines

Automotive Gas Oil (AGO) is specifically formulated for diesel engines, offering excellent fuel efficiency and reduced emissions. Our AGO meets all regulatory standards and is available in various grades.

Crude oil

Raw petroleum for processing and refining

We source and supply high-quality crude oil from various regions, ensuring consistent quality and reliable supply chains. Our crude oil is suitable for various refining processes and industrial applications.

LNG / LPG

Liquefied natural gas and propane

LNG and LPG solutions for industrial and commercial applications. These clean-burning fuels offer environmental benefits and cost efficiency for power generation, heating, and industrial processes.

Jet A1 fuel / Kerosene

Aviation fuel and kerosene products

Jet A1 fuel meets international aviation standards for commercial and private aircraft. Our kerosene products are also available for heating, lighting, and other industrial applications.

RFO / HFO

Residual fuel oil for industrial use

Residual Fuel Oil (RFO) and Heavy Fuel Oil (HFO) are cost-effective solutions for power generation, marine engines, and industrial boilers. We provide various grades to meet specific operational requirements.

Bitumen

Asphalt binder for road construction

High-quality bitumen for road construction, waterproofing, and industrial applications. Our bitumen products meet international standards and are available in various penetration grades.
